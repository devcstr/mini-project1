Mission

Founded in 2019, Laus Group Esports mission has always been to develop, support and recognize players to be professionally competitive in NBA2k internationally. This singular focus on player experience is the driving force behind everything at Laus Group Esports.

Vision

To promote a scenario of respect, equality and ethical values in eSports (national and international), with the aim of becoming a reference in the sector of electronic sports, creating the appropriate ecosystem of normalization and interrelation between players, families and the industry of video games that surrounds it

Core Values

Respect
We encourage mutual respect among colleagues, competitors and professionals in the sector, being together with equality, respect and values, the core of our main identity traits.

Excellence
We promote excellence as one of the main values of our brand identity. For this reason, all the professionals of LGE are working to reach it, with commitment, dedication, humility and illusion, trying this way to obtain the personal, the group and the club objectives. For this same reason, we continue to improve both individually and collectively in each of the different disciplines and their members.

Teamwork
We promote and encourage teamwork between the different areas that are part of the club structure, as well as a good relationship between the different electronic sports sections of LGE Esports. To this purpose, we apply the necessary guidelines and promote them as a differential value and identity trait in order to achieve success.
